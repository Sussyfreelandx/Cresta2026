B2B Email Sign-up

This page is the single, disclosed source of every business email address held by Crestara Construction Autority. Approved contractors, suppliers, vendors, and project partners use this form to opt in to receive operational and contracting updates from us.

Email subscription source statement: all business email addresses on our outbound mailing list are collected through this public sign-up form at /email-subscribe.html on cresteraconstructionauthority.com. Each submission is logged with a timestamp, originating IP address, user-agent, and a copy of the consent text the subscriber agreed to. Logs are retained for the lifetime of the subscription and are used to verify email subscription consent when needed.

Who this is for

  • Approved 1099 contractors and trade companies in our network
  • Material suppliers and equipment vendors quoting on our projects
  • General-contractor and developer partners
  • Insurance, bonding, and compliance professionals working with us

This list is strictly business-to-business. We do not collect or send to consumer addresses, and we do not buy, rent, sell, or share email lists.

What you will receive

  • Open trade-package and subcontract opportunity announcements
  • Compliance, insurance, and OSHA documentation reminders
  • Prequalification windows and approved-contractor renewals
  • Occasional company updates relevant to our contractor network

Typical sending frequency: 1–4 emails per month. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every message or by emailing info@cresteraconstructionauthority.com.

How we handle your data

  • Lawful basis: consent (you tick the box on this page) and legitimate B2B interest in operational contracting communications.
  • Data we record: the form fields you submit, plus a timestamp, your IP address, your browser user-agent, and the URL of this page — the proof-of-consent record requested by SMTP providers.
  • Storage: records are kept by Crestara Construction Autority and are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing.
  • Processors: we use a transactional SMTP provider to deliver the messages you opt in to here; that provider acts only on our instructions.
