Contractor Prequalification Checklist

A public overview of typical compliance items required to maintain Approved Contractor Status.

No work proceeds without a written work order and an approved change order where applicable.

Insurance (typical minimums)

  • General Liability (COI required; $1M minimum)
  • Workers’ Compensation (where applicable)
  • Commercial Auto (where applicable)
  • Umbrella/Excess Liability (project-dependent)

Licensing & Documentation

  • State contractor/trade licensing verification (where required)
  • W-9 submission
  • Business profile and verifiable project experience
  • Legal U.S. work authorization

Safety & Site Requirements

  • OSHA compliance confirmation
  • OSHA 10/30 expectations per trade and jobsite requirements
  • Safety manual acknowledgement
  • Site orientation and supervisor coordination

Scope Governance

  • Scope of Work agreed prior to mobilization
  • Change Orders must be submitted and approved in writing
  • Milestone draws released after verification/inspection
  • Performance rating impacts future allocations

Internal Review Checklist (for Crestara Construction Autority use)

  • Verify COI (GL) and confirm coverage dates/limits
  • Confirm Workers’ Comp, Auto, and Umbrella/Excess where applicable to scope
  • Validate licensing with the applicable state board (where required)
  • Confirm OSHA 10/30 expectations by trade and jobsite
  • Safety manual acknowledgement + site orientation requirements completed prior to mobilization
  • EO / non-discrimination acknowledgement on file
