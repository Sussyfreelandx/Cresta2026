Privacy Policy

We collect information from contractor applicants for compliance review and subcontract allocation purposes.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone number and email
  • Trade category, experience, and operational details
  • Compliance documents (COI, W-9, licensing where applicable)

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Verify eligibility and conduct compliance checks
  • Allocate contract opportunities
  • Maintain contractor records

3. Data Storage

Data is stored on secured systems and retained only as necessary for compliance and operational purposes. Uploaded documents are stored in a secured location and access is limited to authorized staff.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell contractor data. Information may be shared only when legally required or necessary for project execution.

5. Cookies

We may use essential cookies and basic analytics to maintain site functionality and improve performance.

6. Your Rights (U.S.)

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information in accordance with applicable U.S. privacy laws (including CCPA where applicable).

7. No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell personal information.

8. Contact

For privacy enquiries contact: info@cresteraconstructionauthority.com
